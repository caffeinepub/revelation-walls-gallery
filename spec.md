# Specification

## Summary
**Goal:** Build a full-page frontend app that embeds the Revelation Walls Gallery in an iframe with a slim branded attribution bar and a dark, mural-art-inspired visual theme.

**Planned changes:**
- Create a full-viewport iframe embedding `https://t3knologic.github.io/revelation-walls-gallery/` with no border, padding, or outer scrollbars
- Add a slim footer bar crediting: "Revelation Walls – Murals & NFTs at the Dfinity Foundation | Commissioned by Dominic Williams | Painted by Candace Marquette | Built by T3kNo-Logic.xyz"
- Apply a dark gallery theme with warm gold accent colors and bold typography to the footer bar and page background
- Display the mural-inspired banner image above or behind the footer bar as a decorative accent

**User-visible outcome:** Users see the Revelation Walls Gallery filling the entire screen with a non-intrusive branded footer bar crediting the project, artist, commissioner, and app builder, all styled with a dark mural-art aesthetic.
